## Key Notes

- **Roof policy**: FIFA decides whether to close retractable roofs. Historically they close for heat (Qatar precedent). Don't assume open.
- **Kickoff time matters**: Afternoon kicks (12-4pm local) are 5-10°C hotter than evening (7-9pm). Check kickoff time before applying heat edge.
- **2025 Club World Cup was the preview**: Multiple players reported dizziness, "impossible" conditions, "very dangerous." European teams particularly vulnerable.
- **Performance impact data**: Up to 26% less high-speed running, elevated heart rates, quicker fatigue, poorer decision-making, higher injury risk.
