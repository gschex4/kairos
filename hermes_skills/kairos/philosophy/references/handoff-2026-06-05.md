## 2. Golden rule — list, don't guess

To check whether a market exists, **list its series**. A 404 on a hand-built ticker only means your string is malformed; never evidence the market is absent.

## 4. Verified ticker patterns

- KXWCGAME (match winner): event KXWCGAME-26{MMMDD}{T1}{T2}. 3 mkts: -{T1} / -{T2} / -TIE
- KXWCGROUPQUAL (qualify from group): event KXWCGROUPQUAL-26{A..L}; markets -{TEAM}
- KXWCROUND (reach round): event KXWCROUND-26{RO16|QUAR|SEMI|FINAL}; markets -{TEAM}

## 5. Knockout caveat

Group-stage game tickers exist now. **Knockout game tickers do not** — KXWCGAME-26{date}{T1}{T2} needs both team codes, which don't exist until feeding matches resolve. Do NOT precompute or hardcode; re-run discovery after every matchday.
